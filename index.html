About Me

Hi! I am Julia Luo, a China-born and Canada-raised software engineer. I am currently a freshman at UC Berkeley studying towards an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science major, and I love programming and design. Currently, I am proficient in Python, Java, HTML/CSS, and Javascript, and can use Android Studio to create Android apps. I am also familiar with Git, the Microsoft Office series, and Adobe Photoshop.

My less nerdy hobbies include soccer, which I have played competitively up until college, and snowboarding, which is a must for every winter. Also, I love both traditional and digital art, especially acrylic painting and sketching, and can play the piano and bass clarinet.
