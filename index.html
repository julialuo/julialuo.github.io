About Me

Hi! I am Julia Luo, a China-born and Canada-raised software engineer. I am currently a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying towards an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science major, and I love both the software engineering and research sides of computer science. I worked on the YouTube backend team last summer at Google, and will be joining the (much smaller) team at Stripe this summer. Also, I am trying out research this semester in the Berkeley Artificial Research Lab under Pieter Abbeel. I am always looking for opportunities in both industry and research to expand my knowledge and experience!

My less nerdy hobbies include soccer, which I have played competitively up until college, and snowboarding, which is a must for every winter. Also, I love both traditional and digital art, especially acrylic painting and sketching, and can play the piano and bass clarinet.
