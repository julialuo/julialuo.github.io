About Me

Hi! I am Julia Luo, a China-born and Canada-raised software engineer. I am currently a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying towards an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science major, and I love programming and design. I am proficient in Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and Javascript, as well as English, Chinese, and French (okay, well not exactly fluent in the last two). Also, I can use Android Studio and Firebase as well as React Native to create mobile apps.

My less nerdy hobbies include soccer, which I have played competitively up until college, and snowboarding, which is a must for every winter. Also, I love both traditional and digital art, especially acrylic painting and sketching, and can play the piano and bass clarinet.
